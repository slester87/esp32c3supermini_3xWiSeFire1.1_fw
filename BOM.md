# Bill of Materials (BOM)

This list targets a typical Burning Man fire build. Quantities assume a single 3‑channel controller.

## Core Electronics

- ESP32‑C3 Super Mini dev board (1)
  - Example: https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0D4QK5V74
- 3‑channel WiSeFire 1.1 controller PCB (1)
  - Custom board used by this project
- 12V DC power supply (1)
  - Example: https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0D9D5L3B5
- Solenoids, 12V (3)
  - Example: https://www.amazon.com/dp/B00DQ1J4H0

## Plumbing / Gas (Typical Fire Art)

- Fuel source (propane tank + regulator)
- Regulator: Marshall Excelsior MEGR-6120-60 (adjustable high pressure)
  - https://www.amazon.com/dp/B00JNBNEZA
- Pressure gauge (example)
  - https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0BCHPXTB3
- Hoses and fittings rated for propane
- T fitting between regulator and accumulator
  - https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0FH48W44Z
- Emergency cutoff between regulator and the T
  - https://www.amazon.com/dp/B005PYHGYE
- Manifold and fittings to 3 solenoids (T is downstream of the solenoids)
- Flame arrestors and/or flashback protection (recommended)

## Enclosure / Wiring

- Weather‑resistant enclosure sized for PCB + PSU
- Wire (sized for solenoid current), terminal blocks, crimp connectors
- In‑line fuse and holder
- Cable glands or strain reliefs

## Optional / Nice‑to‑Have

- External status LED or light pipe
- Power switch
- Spare solenoids and fittings

## Safety

- Fire extinguisher rated for fuel fires
- Clear safety perimeter and operator spotter
